Decision Tool

Does better seed pay for itself?

Price per bag is the wrong number. Return per acre is the right one. Cold germ score is the number most bag tags don't show — and it's the one that explains yield gaps before the crop ever emerges.

Industry Floor — Corn
85%
Minimum acceptable cold germ. Below this, you're planting at-risk seed into a Midwestern April.
Industry Floor — Soybeans
80%
Standard cold germ threshold. Warm germ standard is 90%.
Yield Drag — Corn
8–15
Est. bu/acre drag from an 85 vs. 92 cold germ gap in adverse conditions.

⚠ Most bag tags don't show cold germination score. Farmers are flying blind on this metric — and the risk is invisible until the crop is in the ground.
